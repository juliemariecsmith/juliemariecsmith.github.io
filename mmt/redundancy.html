The Redundancy Principle

How It Works

If you have ever read along in a text while someone else read that same text out loud, you may have been frustrated by the fact that the narrator was speaking too quickly or too slowly for you. This can make it difficult to follow along. This is the basis for the redundancy principle, which suggests that explanations are provided in text or in audio, but not both.
