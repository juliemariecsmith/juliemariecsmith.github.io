The Coherence Principle

How It Works

Watch the first minute or so of this video, thinking about the music.

Did the music relate to the content? Did the music help you better understand the content? Or was it distracting? For most learners, the music will distract from learning. This is an illustration of the coherence principle, which states that any text, images, video, or sounds that do not contribute to the learning content should be removed from instructional multimedia -- their presence will simply demand the learner's working memory.
