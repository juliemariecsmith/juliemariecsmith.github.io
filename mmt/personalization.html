The Personalization Principle

How It Works

Take a look at this video . . . but prepare to be annoyed.

The idea behind the personalization principle is this: create instructional materials that are closer to spoken language than to written language. So be less formal and more conversational.

Learn More

Read A Personalization Effect in Multimedia Learning: Students Learn Better When Words Are in Conversational Style Rather Than Formal Style, the seminal paper by Richard E. Mayer that explains this principle.
